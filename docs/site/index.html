Free, local-first dictation for macOS.

Download for Mac macOS 13+ · Apple Silicon & Intel Source on GitHub

Murmur is a free, open-source dictation tool that runs Whisper on your laptop. Hold a hotkey, speak, release — your words appear at the cursor in any app. No subscription, no cloud round-trip, no telemetry. The app weighs <200 MB and the smallest model adds another 75 MB.

  • Local first. Runs faster-whisper on your machine. No internet needed.
  • Optional cloud. Plug in a Groq or OpenAI key for faster transcription on long clips.
  • Auto-paste at cursor. Works in browsers, IDEs, terminals, Slack, Notion, Mail.
  • Menu-bar only. No Dock icon, no focus stealing.
